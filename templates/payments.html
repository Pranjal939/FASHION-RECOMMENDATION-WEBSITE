Payment Policy – Trendify

Trendify offers secure and convenient payment options for customers.

Accepted Payment Methods

Customers can make payments using:

  • Credit cards
  • Debit cards
  • UPI payments
  • Cash On Delivery

These payment methods help ensure smooth and fast transactions.

Payment Security

All payments on Trendify are processed through secure payment gateways. Your financial information is encrypted and protected.

Trendify does not store sensitive payment details such as card numbers or PINs.

Order Confirmation

After successful payment:

  • Customers will receive an order confirmation email or SMS.
  • Order details including product name, price, and delivery address will be provided.

Payment Failure

If a payment fails:

  • The order will not be confirmed.
  • Customers can retry the payment or use a different payment method.

Refund Payments

Refunds (if applicable) will be processed through the original payment method used during the purchase.

Refund processing time: 5–10 business days.
