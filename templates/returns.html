Return and Refund Policy – Trendify

Last Updated: 10 March 2026

Customer satisfaction is important to Trendify.

1. Return Eligibility

Products may be returned if:

  • The item is damaged
  • The item is defective
  • The wrong product was delivered

Return requests must be made within 7 days of receiving the product.

2. Return Conditions

To qualify for a return:

  • The product must be unused
  • The product must be in original packaging
  • The customer must provide proof of purchase

3. Refund Process

Once the returned item is received and inspected:

  • Refunds will be processed within 5–10 business days
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method

4. Non-Returnable Items

Certain items may not be eligible for return, such as:

  • Used items
  • Personalized products
  • Final sale items
