Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) – Trendify

1. What is Trendify?

Trendify is an online platform where customers can explore and purchase trendy and high-quality products.

2. How do I create an account?

You can create an account by:

  1. Clicking on the Sign Up button.
  2. Entering your email, phone number, and password.
  3. Verifying your account.

3. How do I place an order?

To place an order:

  1. Browse products on the website.
  2. Add the desired item to your cart.
  3. Proceed to checkout.
  4. Enter shipping details and complete payment.

4. Can I track my order?

Yes. Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS.

5. What should I do if I receive a damaged product?

If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, contact our support team within 7 days of delivery.

6. Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be canceled before they are shipped. After shipping, cancellation may not be possible.

7. How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes:

  • 3–5 days for local orders
  • 5–10 days for national orders

Delivery time may vary depending on location.

8. How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our support team through:

  • Email: support@trendify.com
  • Phone: +91 9313665646
