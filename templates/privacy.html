Privacy Policy – Trendify

Last Updated: 10 March 2026

Trendify values your privacy and aims to protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number)
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Payment information
  • Account login details
  • Website usage information
  • Device and browser information

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Process orders and payments
  • Deliver purchased products
  • Improve website functionality
  • Respond to customer inquiries
  • Send updates, offers, or newsletters
  • Prevent fraud and maintain security

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Trendify may use cookies to improve user experience. Cookies help us:

  • Understand website traffic
  • Remember user preferences
  • Improve website performance

Users can disable cookies in browser settings.

4. Data Protection

Trendify uses appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

5. Sharing Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share data with:

  • Payment processors
  • Delivery partners
  • Website analytics providers

These services only use data necessary to perform their tasks.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access their personal data
  • Request corrections
  • Request deletion of their data

Requests can be made through our contact email.

7. Updates to Privacy Policy

Trendify may modify this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will appear on this page.
