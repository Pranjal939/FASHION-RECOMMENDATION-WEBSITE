Shipping Policy – Trendify

Last Updated: 10 March 2026

Trendify aims to deliver products quickly and securely.

1. Order Processing

Orders are usually processed within 1–3 business days after payment confirmation.

2. Shipping Time

Estimated delivery times:

  • Local deliveries: 3–5 business days
  • National deliveries: 5–10 business days
  • International deliveries (if available): 10–20 business days

Delivery times may vary due to logistics or external factors.

3. Shipping Charges

Shipping costs may vary depending on:

  • Delivery location
  • Order size or weight
  • Ongoing promotions

Shipping fees will be displayed during checkout.

4. Tracking Orders

Once the order is shipped, customers will receive a tracking number via email or SMS.

5. Delays

Trendify is not responsible for delays caused by:

  • Weather conditions
  • Courier company issues
  • Customs processing
